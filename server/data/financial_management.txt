Financial Management
====================

Overview:
Financial management focuses on planning, managing, and monitoring financial resources of a business.

Units:
Unit 1 — Introduction to Finance
- Goals of FM
- Time value of money

Unit 2 — Capital Budgeting
- NPV, IRR
- Payback methods

Unit 3 — Working Capital
- Cash, inventory, receivables

Unit 4 — Cost of Capital
- Debt vs equity
- WACC

Unit 5 — Risk & Return
- CAPM model

Applications:
- Banking
- Investments
- Corporate management
